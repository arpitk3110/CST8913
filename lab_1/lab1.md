### 1.  What are the company's motivators of migration to the cloud?

- Nourish + Bloom Market was motivated to switch to the cloud to support its technology-driven retail model.

- The migration aimed to provide a state-of-the-art, touchless shopping experience for customers.

- Cloud adoption was needed to scale autonomous retail stores efficiently.

- Azure’s security, scalability, and system integration made it a suitable platform for proprietary solutions.

### 2. What questions will you ask to understand more about the company infrastructure? 

To better understand Nourish + Bloom’s infrastructure, I would ask:

- What hardware and software systems are currently used in each of the stores? 
- What is their current data security and compliance practice? 
- How are the transaction volumes and data growth rates expected to grow as they expand?
- What is the current state of the real-time data (inventory, payment, refrigeration monitoring) storage and access?

### 4. Describe the most likely migration approach that will suit the chosen company.

The most suitable approach for Nourish + Bloom is:

- Cloud-native migration is best since systems were designed with modern technologies.

- Azure SaaS and database-as-a-service provide scalability, security, and integration with sensors and robotics.

### 5. Produce a high level schedule for the migration process

- Week 1–2: Assess existing store systems, requirements, and infrastructure.

- Week 3–4: Configure Azure cloud environment, security, and compliance setup.

- Week 5–6: Migrate core applications (checkout, inventory, refrigeration monitoring).

- Week 7–8: Integrate mobile app and delivery robot systems into Azure.

- Week 9–10: Conduct testing, troubleshooting, and employee training.

### 6. What are the main decision criteria for the chosen company? 
Nourish + Bloom’s main decision criteria are:

- Scalability and rapid expansion into new communities with minimal setup.

- Security and compliance for customer payment and inventory data.

- Integration with hardware like cameras, sensors, and delivery robots.

- Cost efficiency and smooth touchless customer experience.
